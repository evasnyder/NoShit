About

"No Shit" was put together within 36 hours at PennApps Winter 2015 at the University of Pennsylvania. The idea came from asking friends and strangers if they could track anything, what would it be. "Trash" was one of them. Then, while eating some delicious sweetish fish italian ice around a trash can soon to be named "Terrance" the idea dawned on the two sleep deprived Mohos.
