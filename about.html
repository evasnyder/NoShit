About

"No Shit" was put together within 36 hours at PennApps Winter 2015 at the University of Pennsylvania. The idea came from asking friends and strangers what kind of inanimate objects they would track if they could track anything. "Trash cans" were one of the responses we got. Naturally, while eating some delicious Swedish fish flavored italian ice around a trashcan, soon to be named "Terrance," this app idea dawned on the two sleep deprived Mohos.
